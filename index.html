Walking the Wall: What No One Tells You

From aching feet to breathtaking views, walking the 84 mile Hadrian’s Wall Path is a nice challenge and a joy. The middle section is remote and wild — coffee is rare, but the silence is golden...but my eyes still see...

Top Tips for First-Time Walkers

  • Train for consecutive long walks and not just one-offs and you will be well prepared
  • Pack light but smart: toiletries, comfy but light clothing, waterproofs, blister plasters and the rest of the first aid kit just in case...
  • Choose the footwear that is right for YOU (boots, trainers, bare feet?!)
  • In summer when everyone likes to get outdoors, make sure to book your accommodation early. I am a big fan of these 2 sites as they both have apps that I keep on my phone. Booking.com Airbnb.co.uk
  • Always have your phone battery charged for emergencies, worth having what3words App installed on your mobile too
